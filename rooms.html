Superior Room

Our Superior Room is thoughtfully designed to offer comfort and convenience with modern amenities. It features a large bed, flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, and a dedicated work area, making it ideal for both relaxation and business needs. The Superior Room is perfect for travelers seeking high-quality accommodation at an affordable price, while enjoying warm and friendly service.

Deluxe Room

The Deluxe Room offers a perfect blend of luxury and comfort. With spacious interiors, it comes with premium amenities such as a large bed, separate bathtub, rain shower, and high-quality bathroom essentials. Additionally, the room boasts a beautiful view of the city or sea, ensuring a unique and memorable stay.
